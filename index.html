About

Hello, I am Sotis, life coach by heart and paws. I was just a regular cat up until a couple of years ago, when I realised people need help. I know the burden of life, when your food is taken away, when nobody listens or even when you're being kidnapped or locked in against your will. I've been there, but I also have come over it. I can help you do the same.

Philosophy

My coaching style is based on three pillars. I let my clients speak for themselves, I listen and don’t interrupt, but I will let you know when it's time to stop. I lead the way but can also follow, you will find your path but I will let you know if it's the wrong one. And I will challenge you, streching the body streches the mind. Follow my lead to a more supple body.
