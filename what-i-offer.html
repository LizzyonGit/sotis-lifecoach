What I offer

A session can focus on different parts of life. Here you can read about the different treatments I offer. Sessions primarily take place at my beautiful place in Soticity.

Book a session here
Talking

Pour your heart out in a deep meaningful conversation in a peaceful environment. You can talk about whatever you want or need. I am listening.

Duration: 2 hours
Price: 1 chicken filet

Exercise

Get your much needed work-out together with me. We start with stretching and yoga to loosen up the muscles. Then we do high-intensity training outside.

Duration: 30 minutes
Price: 2 liquid snacks

Walking

We take a nice walk in the nature. On the way, we might climb some trees as this is very beneficial for your mood!

Duration: 1 hour
Price: 1 fish of your choice

Combination

This is a session consisting of exercise, a nature walk and talking therapy.

Duration: 3 hours
Price: 1/2 chicken
